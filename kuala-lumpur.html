Itinerary

Día 1: Llegada y Bienvenida
Día 2-3: Destacados de la Ciudad
Día 4-5: Inmersión Cultural
Día 6-7: Maravillas Naturales
Día 8-12: Ocio y Exploración
Día 13: Despedida
Día 14: Salida

Day 1: Arrival and Welcome

Meet and greet at the hotel. Evening city walk and welcome dinner.

Day 2-3: City Highlights

Tour of the Petronas Towers, visit to Merdeka Square, and cultural shows.

Day 4-5: Cultural Immersion

Spend these days exploring the rich history and art of Kuala Lumpur. Visit the National Museum, participate in a batik painting workshop, and enjoy an evening at the Malaysian Philharmonic Orchestra.

Day 6-7: Natural Wonders

Discover the breathtaking landscapes of Kuala Lumpur with a trip to the Kuala Lumpur Bird Park and a hike in the Bukit Bintang area to see the city's natural flora and fauna.

Day 8-12: Leisure and Exploration

These days are reserved for personal leisure and further exploration. Take your time shopping at the Suria KLCC, explore local markets, or enjoy a day trip to nearby attractions such as the Genting Highlands.

Day 13: Farewell

Join your fellow travelers for a farewell feast at one of Kuala Lumpur's renowned rooftop restaurants, where you'll dine with panoramic views of the city as you reminisce about your journey.

Day 14: Departure

After a leisurely breakfast, take some time for last-minute shopping or photo opportunities before your transfer to the airport for your departure.

Travel Tips

Remember to wear comfortable and weather-appropriate clothing for the tropical climate.

Try the delicious street food of Kuala Lumpur.

Bring mosquito repellent for jungle excursions.

Explore the city's nightlife.

For budget dining, check out local food stalls and hawker centers. They offer affordable and tasty options.

Don't forget to haggle when shopping at local markets for souvenirs and gifts.

Pack light and versatile clothing for your trip. Consider quick-dry fabrics for outdoor activities.

Bring a good pair of walking shoes for exploring the city and comfortable sandals for hot days.

Carry a reusable water bottle to stay hydrated, and use eco-friendly bags for your purchases.
